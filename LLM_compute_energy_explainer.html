The slider illustrates within-model scaling only, calibrated at ~0.0015 Wh per generated token (so a ~200-token answer ≈ 0.3 Wh); actual consumption depends on model size, hardware, and serving stack. Reference points are independently sourced estimates for the specific models measured: median Gemini at 0.24 Wh (Google, 2025); GPT-4o ~0.3 Wh (Epoch AI, 2025); Claude 3.7 at 0.84–17 Wh and DeepSeek-R1 at 2.25–33.6 Wh (Jegham et al., 2025; IEA). Measurement boundaries differ, so values are indicative and not strictly comparable across models.
